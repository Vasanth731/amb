Natural Science

All these which we saw above is formal science and this opens the doors to natural science which has categories like physical, life and planetary. The physical deals with the classical physics like mechanics, thermodynamics and electromagnetism. This opens up chemistry which is split into physical chemistry, inorganic & organic chemistry, which automatically gives rise to biology which is a life science and then to planetary science, which speaks about stars and the universe which is the earth & cosmology.
