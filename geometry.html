Geometry

Geometry is the branch of mathematics concerned with shapes, sizes, positions, and properties of space. From ancient Greek origins to modern differential geometry, it studies points, lines, surfaces, and solids, exploring their relationships and measurements.

Euclidean geometry deals with flat spaces, while non-Euclidean geometries explore curved spaces. Geometry is fundamental to architecture, art, astronomy, and understanding the fabric of spacetime itself in modern physics.
