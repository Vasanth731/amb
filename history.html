History

History is the study of past events, particularly in human affairs. Through examining documents, artifacts, and oral traditions, historians piece together narratives of how civilizations rose and fell, how ideas spread and evolved, and how the actions of individuals and groups shaped the world.

Understanding history helps us comprehend present circumstances, learn from past mistakes, and make informed decisions about the future. It reveals patterns of human behavior, social change, and the forces that drive civilizations forward or lead to their decline.
