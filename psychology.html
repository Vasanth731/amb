Psychology

Psychology is the scientific study of the mind and behavior, encompassing conscious and unconscious mental processes, emotions, and actions. Structuralism, functionalism and Psychoanalysis. It’s our brain and how its connected through the nervous system by the neurons and neuro-transmitters like the endorphins and other hormones cause all our brain activity and feelings. Perception is the top-down way our brains organize and interpret the information and put it into context. Consciousness is our awareness of ourselves and our environment. Rapid Eye Movement (REM) is the recurring sleep stage during which vivid dreams commonly occur where the motor cortex is active but the brainstem is blocking those messages such that it gives the muscles the feel of paralysis, except for the eyes. Sensory memories to short term(working memory) to long term (procedural, mnemonics, chunking) which is made strong by recall. The explicit memory is willingly concentrated memory while implicit is retention independent of conscious recollection. Cognition is our thoughts, perspectives and expectations. Language is a set of spoken, written or signed words which combine together to communicate meaning. Ego gets what the unconscious energy wants balancing with the superego. In psychoanalytic theory, defense mechanisms is the ego’s protective method of reducing anxiety by unconsciously distorting reality. This defense mechanism is part of our personality. Social cognitive perspective is the interaction between our traits and their social context. The Schachter-Singer theory states that to experience emotion one must be physically aroused and cognitively label that arousal. Emotions 2D model - (plesant/positive) relaxed, enthusiastic, sad, angry (unplesant/negative). Psychological Disorders and mental illness seems to first cause Distress (subjective feeling that something is wrong) which then leads to Dysfunction (inability to work). OCD (Obsessive Compulsive Disorder), Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD), Phobias, Panic attacks are few examples. It is caused by learning perspective and biological perspective. Depressive and Bipolar Disorders, Trauma and Addiction, Schizophrenia and Dissociative Disorder, Personality disorders are few other examples. Psychotheraphy involves a therapist using a range of techniques to help overcome troubles, gain insight and achieve personal growth is an initial step to treat disorders. Biomedical Treatments ain to physiologically change the brain’s electrochemical state with psychotropic drugs, magnetic impulses or electrical currents. Pharmacotheraphy uses drugs to affect the mental state. They are categorized into four categories, antipsychotics, anxiolytics, antidepressants, mood stabilizers. In individual psychology there are three branches cognitive, personality and clinica. While social psychology focuses on the power of the situation and how each one influences one another in certain condition. Cooperation is the key. Stereotype is an overgeneralized belief about a particular group of people. Combination of biological factors like genetic, neurological, biochemical influences, environment and experience add on to how we behave.
