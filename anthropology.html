Anthropology

Studies on the human culture from a history and evolutionary perspective. The four branches are cultural, archaeology, biological and linguistic. We understand about people who lived years ago through ruins and objects they left behind. It also helps us to understand the cultures around the world. In the The Brief History of Humankind Yuval Noah Harari states that the cognitive, agricultural and scientific revolution are the main few revolutions in the human history. By examining social practices, belief systems, languages, human evolution, and material remains, anthropology helps us build a comprehensive picture of how societies develop and adapt over time.
