Sociology

Studies about people and society work in the present. It focuses on social institutions and behavior patterns. It covers both the macro and micro focus of asking the questions regarding society. Social functions are of two types manifest (intended consequences of particular structure) and latent (unintended). It’s like how schools are for learning as well as for socializing. Social dysfunctions also exist. Aguste Comte, the founder of sociology states that phenomena can be studied through direct observation and these can be pulled together into theories. This is used to make a data driven reseach to analyze and the hypotheses (possible relationship between two variables). The conflict theory states that we labour and change things around to survive and while trying to free ourselves from these natural constrains we entangled ourselves in new social constraints. It also deals with theories like Race Conflict theory and Gender conflict theory. Symbols, values and norms are different in different senarios which leads us to a culture, subculture and counter culture. Nurture is the behaviour based on the people and environment around us. Social development, socialization, interactions led us from small structures to larger structures. Social group is a collection of people who have something in common and significant. Formal organizations are formed to achieve goals efficiently while Noemative organizations are formed for people to join as volunteers and pursue some goal that is morally worthwile and Coercive organisations are those like jail where people don’t have a say in joining or not. An oligarchy is where the people in the top make decision for all in the botton like a hierarchy which gives rise to bureaucracies. Deviants are non-normative, positive and negative sanctions are response to conformity. Social stratification is a system by which society categorizes people and ranks them in hierarchy. Aparthed and caste system are few examples. Anticipatory socialization is learning to fit into a group you’ll someday be a part of. Class socialization is the most common one passed on from parents to children. Politics, Sex, Race, Ethnicity, Religion all of these can be understood through the concepts of sociology. Gender is social convention while sex is binary. Ethnicities are socially constructed categories based on cultural traits, also that has a shared cultural heritage but not on visual or biological traits. Race is based on observable, physical traits. Kinship is a social bond which is based on common ancestry, marriage or adoption. Fictive kin is when friends become family. Education is a social institution through which society provides its members with all kinds of important knowledge.
