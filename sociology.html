Sociology

Sociology is the systematic study of human society, social relationships, institutions, and collective behavior. It examines how social structures and forces shape individual lives, how groups interact, and how societies maintain order and experience change.

Sociologists investigate topics like social inequality, family structures, education, religion, crime, and social movements. Through research methods ranging from surveys to ethnography, sociology reveals patterns in social life and helps us understand contemporary social issues.
