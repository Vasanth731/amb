Planetary Sciences

Planetary sciences encompass Earth science, astronomy, and cosmology, studying planets, moons, stars, galaxies, and the universe itself. From understanding our home planet's geology and atmosphere to exploring distant worlds and the cosmos, these sciences reveal our place in the universe.

Earth science studies our planet's processes and history, astronomy explores celestial objects and phenomena, and cosmology investigates the origin, evolution, and ultimate fate of the universe. Together, they answer fundamental questions about where we came from and where we're going.
