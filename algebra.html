Algebra

Algebra serves as a gateway to mathematics. Patterns and problems can be written symbolically and solved using different algebraic concepts. Natural patterns such as flower petals are often described through sequences and series.

Arithmetic progression: \[ a_n = a + (n-1)d \] \[ S_n = \frac{n}{2}[2a + (n-1)d] \]

Geometric progression: \[ a_n = ar^{n-1} \] \[ S_n = a\left(\frac{1-r^n}{1-r}\right) \]

Harmonic progression is the reciprocal of an arithmetic progression.

Permutations and combinations: \[ nP_r = \frac{n!}{(n-r)!} \] \[ nP_r = r! \times nC_r \]

Probability and statistics describe uncertainty in events.

\[ P(A|B) = \frac{P(A \cap B)}{P(B)} \] where \[ P(A) = \frac{n(A)}{n(S)} \]

Standard deviation: \[ \sigma = \sqrt{\frac{\sum x^2}{n} - \left(\frac{\sum x}{n}\right)^2} \] or \[ \sigma = \sqrt{\frac{\sum d^2}{n}} \] where \[ d = x - \bar{x}, \quad \bar{x} = \frac{\sum x}{n} \] and variance \(= \sigma^2\).

Logarithms and the Binomial Theorem introduce functions.

\[ (x+y)^n = {n \choose r} x^{n-r}y^r \] \[ \log_a b = \frac{\log_c b}{\log_c a} \]

Quadratic equations:

\[ x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} \]

Discriminant conditions:

\[ D = 0 \text{ (real and equal roots)} \] \[ D > 0 \text{ (real and distinct roots)} \] \[ D < 0 \text{ (complex roots)} \]

Complex numbers:

\[ z = a + ib \] \[ \bar{z} = a - ib \] \[ |z| = \sqrt{a^2 + b^2} \] \[ z = re^{i\theta} \] where \[ \theta = \arg z = \tan^{-1}\left(\frac{b}{a}\right) \] \[ z = r(\cos\theta + i\sin\theta) \]

Trigonometry describes the relationships in triangles and pyramidal structures.

\[ \sin\theta = \frac{O}{H}, \quad \cos\theta = \frac{A}{H}, \quad \tan\theta = \frac{O}{A} \]

Matrices and determinants:

\[ A^{-1} = \frac{1}{|A|}\,\mathrm{adj}\,A \] \[ \mathrm{adj}\,A = (\mathrm{cof}\,A)^T \] \[ AX = B \] \[ X = A^{-1}B \]
