Philosophy

Philosophy is the love of wisdom, asking fundamental questions about reality, knowledge, values, reason, mind, and language. It employs critical thinking and logical argument to examine assumptions, clarify concepts, and explore the nature of existence, truth, beauty, justice, and the good life.

Metaphysics investigates the nature of reality, epistemology explores knowledge and belief, ethics examines moral values and right action, and logic studies valid reasoning. Philosophy provides foundational questions and methods that underlie all academic disciplines and human inquiry.
