Life Sciences

Life sciences, primarily biology, explore living organisms and their vital processes. From molecular biology studying DNA and proteins to ecology examining ecosystems, life sciences reveal the incredible complexity and diversity of life on Earth.

Through studying genetics, evolution, physiology, and ecology, we understand how life emerges, adapts, reproduces, and interacts within ecosystems. Life sciences drive medical advances, biotechnology, and our understanding of our place in the natural world.
