Physical Sciences

$s(t) = 5t^{2} + 2t + 1$ $v(t) = 10t + 2$ $a(t) = 10$ $\frac{ds}{dt} = v$ $\frac{dv}{dt} = \frac{d^{2}s}{dt^{2}} = a$ 1st law states that the object remains in constant motion until acted upon by external force. $p = mv$, \quad $\frac{dp}{dt} = m\frac{dv}{dt}$ $a =$ constant then $\frac{dv}{dt} = 0$ $\therefore F = 0$. This is the law of inertia. 2nd law is law of acceleration. $p = mv$, \quad $\frac{dp}{dt} = m\frac{dv}{dt}$ $a = \frac{F}{m}$ The 3rd law is law of action reaction. $p_{AB} + p_{BA} = m_A v_A + m_B v_B \rightarrow \frac{dp_{AB}}{dt} + \frac{dp_{BA}}{dt} = m_A \frac{dv_A}{dt} + m_B \frac{dv_B}{dt}$ $F_{AB} = -F_{BA}$ The equations of motion are as follows $a = \frac{dv}{dt}$ $\int_{v_0}^{v} dv = \int a\,dt$ $v - v_0 = at \rightarrow v = v_0 + at$ $v = \frac{ds}{dt}$ $v_0 + at = \frac{ds}{dt} \rightarrow \int v_0 dt + \int at\,dt = \int ds$ $s = v_0 t + \frac{at^2}{2}$ $a = \frac{dv}{dt} = \frac{dv}{ds}\frac{ds}{dt} = v\frac{dv}{ds}$ $\int a\,ds = \int v\,dv \rightarrow as = \frac{v^2}{2} - \frac{v_0^2}{2}$ $v_0^2 + 2as = v^2$ These concepts get extended to rotational mechanics as well. And then comes work, energy & power. $W = \int_A^B F \cdot ds$ $P = F \times V$ $E_{KE} = \frac{1}{2}mv^2$ $E_{PE} = mgh$ Kinetic theory of gases explain $PV = nRT$ Fluid dynamics explain $P = \frac{F}{A}$ $P_1A_1V_1 = P_2A_2V_2$ Then comes waves & oscillations $p(x,t) = A \sin(kx - \omega t + \phi)$ and optics $\frac{1}{u} + \frac{1}{v} = \frac{1}{f}$ & gravitation $F = \frac{G M m}{r^2}$ and we move into thermodynamics $dQ = dU + dW$ (heat) \quad (internal energy) \quad (work done by system) Now comes the most used concept in our regular life, the electromagnetism. The four equations beautifully describe light. $\nabla \cdot \mathbf{B} = 0$ ; $\nabla \cdot \mathbf{D} = \rho_v$ ; $\nabla \times \mathbf{E} = -\frac{\partial \mathbf{B}}{\partial t}$ $\nabla \times \mathbf{H} = \mathbf{J} + \frac{\partial \mathbf{D}}{\partial t}$ Then comes the modern physics which unlock the quantum realm. Atoms break further into hadrons (baryons & mesons) and leptons. $u, c, t$ \qquad $\bar{u}, \bar{c}, \bar{t}$ $d, s, b$ \qquad $\bar{d}, \bar{s}, \bar{b}$ quarks & its antiparticles. $e^{-}, \mu^{-}, \tau^{-}$ \qquad $e^{+}, \mu^{+}, \tau^{+}$ $\nu_e, \nu_\mu, \nu_\tau$ \qquad $\bar{\nu}e, \bar{\nu}\mu, \bar{\nu}_\tau$ leptons & its antiparticles. $\gamma, W^{+}, W^{-}, Z^{0}, g, H^{0}$ Bosons. Feynman diagram. Proton & neutron form the nucleus and surrounded by electron gives rise to all forms of creation in this universe.
